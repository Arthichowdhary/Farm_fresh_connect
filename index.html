About HarvestHub

Our Mission

HarvestHub was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to create a direct connection between local farmers and consumers. We believe that everyone deserves access to fresh, healthy, and sustainably grown food, while farmers deserve fair compensation for their hard work.

Our Story

Our founder, Rohan Mehta, grew up visiting his grandparents' farm in Punjab. He saw firsthand the challenges farmers faced in getting their products to market and receiving fair prices. After studying agricultural economics, he created HarvestHub to bridge this gap using technology.

Our Impact

Since our launch, we've connected over 500 farmers with more than 10,000 customers across India. We've helped increase farmer incomes by an average of 30% while providing customers with fresher produce at lower prices than traditional retail channels.

Our Values

  • Sustainability: We promote organic farming practices that protect our environment
  • Transparency: We believe you should know where your food comes from and how it's grown
  • Fairness: We ensure farmers receive fair compensation for their products
  • Quality: We deliver the freshest, highest-quality products directly from farm to table
